Complete Feature Overview

🧠 State-of-the-Art AI

Powered by HTDemucs (Meta AI) for studio-quality music separation and Whisper AI for highly accurate, multilingual lyric extraction.

🎚️ Real-time Mixer

Interactive audio mixing right in your browser. Adjust volumes, mute specific instruments, or solo a track while following the interactive waveforms.

💾 Advanced Exporting

Download separated stems individually, grab them all as a ZIP file, or dynamically mix selected tracks into a brand new high-quality WAV file.

⚡ WebGPU Acceleration

Leverages your computer's graphics card directly through the browser for lightning-fast AI processing, with WASM fallback for older devices.

🎙️ Live Studio Recording

Don't just separate music—create it. Sing over uploaded backing tracks with your microphone, and export the final mixed song instantly.

📦 Zero Server Processing

Your files never leave your computer. We download the AI models to your browser's local cache once, ensuring complete privacy and offline capabilities.
