Complete Feature Overview

🧠 State-of-the-Art AI

Powered by HTDemucs (Meta AI) for studio-quality music separation and Whisper AI for highly accurate, multilingual lyric extraction.

🎚️ Real-time Mixer

Interactive audio mixing right in your browser. Adjust volumes, mute specific instruments, or solo a track while following the interactive waveforms.

🎛️ Advanced Audio FX Suite

Apply 35+ professional audio effects (Reverb, Auto-Tune, EQ, Compression, Distortion, etc.) directly to individual stems. Includes 70+ quick presets like Concert Hall, Radio Ready, Robot Voice, and Tape Saturation. Effects are applied in real-time and baked in when downloading.

💾 Advanced Exporting

Download separated stems individually, grab them all as a ZIP file, or dynamically mix selected tracks into a custom WAV file using your current volume, mute settings, and active effects.

Leverages your computer's graphics card directly through the browser for lightning-fast AI processing, with WASM fallback for older devices.

🎙️ Live Studio Recording

Don't just separate music—create it. Sing over uploaded backing tracks with your microphone, and export the final mixed song instantly.

🔒 Zero Server Processing

Your files never leave your computer. We download the AI models to your browser's local cache once, ensuring complete privacy and offline capabilities.

⏱️ Precision Export Trimming

Define custom start and end times to export only a specific segment of your separated stems or custom mix, without modifying the underlying audio buffers.

✂️ Regional Audio Editing

Perform non-destructive edits directly on the waveforms. Instantly Isolate or Mute specific time regions of individual stems with full Undo support.

🎛️ Full DAW Studio Mode

Immerse yourself in a full-screen Digital Audio Workstation environment. Manage separations, lyrics, multi-track recordings, and batch merging from a single unified interface.

🔗 Batch Audio Merger

Drop multiple audio files into the Merger dashboard to automatically stitch them together into a seamless single track with zero latency.

💾 AI Model Caching

Our advanced IndexedDB wrapper intelligently caches gigabytes of ONNX model files locally. You only wait for the download once, enabling instant reuse forever.

🔄 Perfect Time Alignment

Our recording engine captures global playback offset down to the millisecond. Your overdubbed vocals always align perfectly with the backing tracks during playback and mixdown.

Quick Documentation

🚀 Getting Started

1. Import: Drag and drop any audio file into the Dashboard.
2. Wait for AI: The AI models will download on the first run. This is a one-time operation. Future separations will be immediate.
3. Mix & Match: Once separated, use the Stems interface to mute, solo, or export individual tracks or custom mixes.

🎙️ Recording Studio

Click "Record & Add Track" to open the Studio. Hit the red Start button to record your microphone over the backing tracks. The system precisely tracks your time offset. When finished, hit Download Custom Mix to render your vocals flawlessly into the master track without any sync issues.

✂️ Editing & Trimming

Use the Export Region inputs to define a start and end boundary. You can then use the Isolate button to silence everything outside the region, or the Mute button to silence only the region itself. Use Undo if you make a mistake.

🔗 Track Merger

Click "Open Audio Merger" to combine multiple files into a single track sequentially. Drag and drop files, drag to reorder them in the list, and hit Merge Tracks. This happens instantly in the browser without any re-encoding latency.

📝 AI Lyrics Extraction

Powered by Whisper AI. Click "Extract Lyrics" and choose your target language. The system will transcribe the vocals locally using WebGPU and provide a timestamped lyrics file (.txt) alongside on-screen playback tracking.

💾 Local Caching System

StemSplit utilizes an advanced IndexedDB caching layer. The ~1.5GB Demucs and Whisper AI models are only downloaded once. Subsequent visits to the app will instantly load the models from your hard drive, allowing 100% offline usage.
